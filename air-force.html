The Afika Air-Force project is an open-source project focused on rapid prototyping a low-cost and 3D printed ventilator using readily available, affordable components to support medical facilities during the COVID-19 crisis.

We have currently developed a Proof of Concept of our ventilator and are in the process of rapid prototyping.

We have completed a full Fusion 360 design of our first prototype to manufacture

We are in need of access to the following items in the western cape during this lock-down period to build the physical prototype:
  • - A laser cutter to cut 1mm stainless steel.
  • - A 3D printer to 3D print PLA or ABS
  • - 250mm PVC tubing
  • - 25mm acrylic or PVC tubing
  • - 18mm O-Rings
  • - A medical staff member that can verify our prototype will work and give input on the design.
