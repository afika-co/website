The Afika Air-Force project is an open-source project focused on rapid prototyping a low-cost and 3D printed ventilator using readily available, affordable components to support our local clinics in the COVID-19 crisis.

We have currently developed a Proof of Concept of our ventilator and are in the process of rapid prototyping.
