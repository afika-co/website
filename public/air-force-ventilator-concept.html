who we are

The Afika Air-Force project is an open-source project focused on rapid prototyping at a low-cost and 3D printed ventilator using readily affordable components to support medical facilities during COVID-19 crisis.

proof of concept

Our aim is to design a ventilator that can be used in the most remote locations. We have currently developed a Proof of Concept of our ventilator and are in the process of rapid prototyping.
