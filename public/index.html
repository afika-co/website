who we are

Afika.co is a non-profit umbrella project of local solutions
in response to the current COVID-19 crisis in South Africa.

what we do

We are working to ensure that we can #SlowTheSpread of COVID-19. From face masks that offer the perfect balance of breathability and efficient filtering to our Air-Force ventilator concept, to our translation efforts to spread facts faster than the virus, we are working tirelessly to equip us all with the tools we'll need to win the battle against the Novel Coronavirus pandemic.
